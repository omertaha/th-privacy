CATE – Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: January 2026

How to delete your account inside the app

  1. Open CATE and tap the Profile tab.
  2. Tap Settings → scroll to the Account section.
  3. Choose Delete Account and confirm the dialog.
  4. The app immediately removes your profile data and signs you out. You can close the app once you see the login screen.

Tip: If Firebase asks for a “recent login,” sign in again first, then repeat the steps above.

What happens when you delete

  • Deleted immediately: profile information (name, gender, birth date, location, industries, education, experience, bio, intent score), discovery preferences, uploaded photos in Google Cloud Storage, cached discovery decks on your device, and your Firebase Authentication record.
  • Chat visibility: once the deletion finishes you no longer appear in matches; existing conversations become inaccessible to others.
  • Retention: only minimal backend audit logs that we must keep for security or legal reasons may remain for up to 30 days. Those logs cannot be used to recreate your account.

Need help without app access?

Email us at cate@cateapp.com from the address on file. Include:

  • Your full name and the email/phone tied to the account
  • “CATE account deletion request” in the subject line

After verifying ownership we run the same deletion flow described above and confirm via email.
